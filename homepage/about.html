About Sasha

Sasha, sometimes you get stuck in one emotion or in one particular issue. Then you become overwhelmed, your life fragmented and you no longer see the whole picture.

This site’s purpose is to help you to see, that your life contains multitude of parts. Parts that you can be thankful that you have. Parts you can be proud that you’ve made or deserved. Parts that are caring about you. There are parts where you can ask for help, even if you not used to. And parts that are asking you to care about them. Parts that are to be maintained. And some parts that are waiting to be changed.

You can come and see them when confused.

If you continue to keep this place alive.
